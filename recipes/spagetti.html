Spagetti Recipe

Home

Description

Spagetti is an Italian pasta dish consisting of thin strands of spagetti, tomato sauce, and other garnishes

Ingredients

  • Spagetti
  • Tomato sauce
  • Grated cheese
  • Meatballs

Steps

  1. Boil pasta to al dente
  2. Cook meatballs until fully cooked
  3. Plate spagetti on a flate plate
  4. Add tomato sauce on top of spagetti
  5. Add meatballs on top of tomato sauce
  6. Grate cheese on top of everything
