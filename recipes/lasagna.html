Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

Lasgna is an Italian pasta dish consisting of alternating layers of pasta, cheese, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Grated cheese

Steps

  1. Cut pasta sheets to size of pan
  2. Alternate layers of pasta, tomato sauce, pasta, and cheese
  3. Repeat until desiered height is reached, usually 5 layers
